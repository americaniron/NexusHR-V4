Video Agent now generates cinematic footage around your Digital Twin. What this means in practice: one prompt gives you a complete video with your AI avatar in cinematic shots that move and feel like they were professionally produced.

## **How to use it**

Open Video Agent, type your prompt, and generate. Seedance footage will automatically be built around your Digital Twin. No additional steps required.

## **Why we built this**

Video Agent was already the fastest way to go from a prompt to a finished video. Seedance makes the A-roll footage feel cinematic. Together, they close the gap between having an idea and having something genuinely worth sharing.

## **Known limitations**

Seedance generations are up to 12 seconds per clip inside the general purpose generator. Video Agent stitches these together to create videos up to three minutes long. Generation time may be longer than standard Video Agent videos.
