# NexsusHR Product Demo Video Script

**Tone:** Professional, aspirational, confident. Think Apple keynote meets enterprise SaaS demo.
**Music:** Modern ambient corporate (builds energy gradually)

**VISUAL:** Cinematic montage — a bustling modern office. Real employees at desks. Then — quick cuts to AI professional profile cards appearing on screens: Sarah Chen (Customer Support Lead), Marcus Wright (Senior Data Analyst), Alex Kim (DevOps Engineer). Green "Online" indicators pulse on each.

**VOICEOVER:**
"What if you could hire a world-class professional — with deep expertise, a real personality, and the ability to work autonomously — in under 60 seconds? No interviews. No onboarding. No six-figure salary. Meet NexsusHR."

### SCENE 2: THE LANDING PAGE & SIGNUP (0:20 - 0:55)

**VISUAL:** Screen recording of the NexsusHR landing page. Camera slowly pans across the hero section showing the 8 AI professionals with their photos, names, and roles. The looping background video shows offices and command centers. The cursor clicks "Start Free Trial."

**VOICEOVER:**
"Getting started takes less than a minute. Visit NexsusHR and you'll immediately see the AI workforce — real professionals with faces, names, and specializations across 105 roles."

**VISUAL:** The signup screen appears. A user enters their email and creates an account. The organization setup screen follows — enter company name, industry, and team size.

**VOICEOVER:**
"Sign up with your work email — no credit card required. You'll get a full 14-day free trial with Growth-tier access: up to 10 AI Professionals, 200 voice hours, 25,000 messages, and 50 automated workflows. Set up your organization in seconds — just your company name, industry, and team size."

### SCENE 3: THE TALENT HUB — FINDING YOUR AI PROFESSIONAL (0:55 - 1:50)

**VISUAL:** The Talent Hub (marketplace) loads. A grid of AI professional cards with avatars, names, departments, seniority badges, and ratings. The user types "financial analyst" in the search bar. Results filter in real-time. They click on "Financial Analyst AI" — Priya Patel's profile opens.

**VOICEOVER:**
"Welcome to the Talent Hub — your marketplace for AI professionals. Browse over 105 specialized roles across engineering, marketing, finance, healthcare, legal, operations, and more. Search by role, department, or industry. Each professional comes with a detailed profile."

**VISUAL:** The role detail page shows: full description, core responsibilities, daily/weekly/monthly tasks, supported tools and integrations (SAP, Tableau, Bloomberg, Slack), data access permissions, personality defaults (formality, empathy, directness), and example workflows.

**VOICEOVER:**
"Every role has been meticulously designed with real-world expertise. You'll see their core responsibilities, the tools they integrate with — like SAP, Salesforce, Jira, or Slack — their data access permissions, personality traits, and example workflows they can run autonomously. The platform helps you find the right fit by matching roles to your industry, team structure, and the specific problems you need solved."

### SCENE 4: HIRING YOUR AI PROFESSIONAL (1:50 - 2:40)

**VISUAL:** The user clicks "Hire This Professional." A hiring dialog appears. They enter a name: "Priya." They select a voice from a dropdown (warm, professional female voice). They click "Generate Avatar" — a photorealistic headshot generates in 3 seconds. Personality sliders appear: Formality (8/10), Enthusiasm (6/10), Empathy (7/10), Directness (8/10). The user adjusts Enthusiasm up to 7.

**VOICEOVER:**
"When you find the right role, hiring is instant. Give your AI professional a name, choose their voice, and generate a unique photorealistic avatar. Then fine-tune their personality — adjust formality, enthusiasm, empathy, directness, humor, and more. Make them match your company culture exactly. This isn't a one-size-fits-all tool. This is your colleague, built to fit your team."

**VISUAL:** The user clicks "Confirm Hire." A brief animation shows the professional being added to the organization. A success message: "Priya has joined your organization."

**VOICEOVER:**
"Click hire, and Priya joins your organization immediately. No waiting. No training period. She's ready to work."

### SCENE 5: YOUR AI PROFESSIONAL AT WORK — THE COMMAND CENTER (2:40 - 3:30)

**VISUAL:** The Command Center (dashboard) shows the organization's AI workforce. Cards for each hired professional show their status (Active), current task, messages handled, and performance metrics. The user clicks on Priya's card.

**VOICEOVER:**
"Your Command Center gives you a real-time overview of your entire AI workforce. See who's active, what they're working on, and how they're performing — all in one place."

**VOICEOVER:**
"Start a conversation via chat or voice. Ask Priya to build a Q4 forecast — she'll analyze the data, produce structured outputs, and offer strategic recommendations. Every interaction is powered by a state-of-the-art language model. No scripted responses. No simulation. Real reasoning."

**VISUAL:** The user assigns a recurring task: "Weekly P&L Variance Report — every Monday at 9 AM." Priya acknowledges and the task appears in the task dashboard.

**VOICEOVER:**
"Assign recurring tasks, and your AI professional handles them autonomously. Weekly reports, daily monitoring, monthly audits — they execute on schedule, every time."
